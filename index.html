About Us

Welcome to Hotel Vishwanath Palace, a premier dining destination located in the heart of Pune, Wakad. Our restaurant is celebrated for its exceptional blend of gourmet cuisine and elegant ambiance, making it an ideal choice for both casual dining and special occasions. With a menu featuring a diverse array of dishes, top-notch service, and a stylish setting, we ensure that every guest enjoys a memorable dining experience.

Situated in the vibrant neighborhood of Wakad, our restaurant is conveniently located near major business hubs, shopping centers, and local attractions. Whether you're here for a business lunch, a family dinner, or a romantic evening out, you'll find everything you need within easy reach.

At Hotel Vishwanath Palace, we take pride in our warm hospitality and attention to detail. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing personalized service to cater to your every need. From our exquisite menu options to our inviting atmosphere, we offer a wide range of dining experiences designed to delight your senses.

Experience the perfect blend of culinary excellence and sophisticated charm at Hotel Vishwanath Palace, where your dining pleasure is our top priority. We look forward to welcoming you and ensuring you have an unforgettable dining experience in Pune.
